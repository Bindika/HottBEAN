  • Designing and creating graphics, icons, and other visual elements to enhance the user experience and communicate information effectively.
  • Conducting usability testing and user research to gather feedback and improve website design.
  • Incorporating branding and marketing elements into website design to ensure consistency with the organization's overall image and messaging.

QALIFICATION:

  • A bachelor's degree in graphic design, visual communication, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, or Sketch.
