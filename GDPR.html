GDPR 2018

Hot Beans company takes the protection of personal data seriously and adheres to the requirements of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Here are some of the measures Hot Beans takes to protect personal data:>

  • We ensures that personal data is processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner. This means that individuals are informed about the processing of their personal data and the reasons why it is being processed.
  • Collects personal data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes, and does not further process it in a way that is incompatible with those purposes and only collects personal data that is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary in relation to the purposes for which it is processed.
  • Hot Beans takes steps to ensure that personal data is accurate and up-to-date. Inaccurate or outdated data is corrected or erased without delay.
  • We keeps personal data in a form that permits identification of data subjects for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which the personal data is processed.
  • Hot Beans takes appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure that personal data is processed in a manner that ensures appropriate security, including protection against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Overall, Hot Beans is committed to protecting personal data and ensuring compliance with the GDPR. Hot Beans understands the importance of maintaining the trust of its customers and takes its data protection obligations seriously.
